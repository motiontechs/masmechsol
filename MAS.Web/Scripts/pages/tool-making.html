Tool Making

MAS Mechsol is one of best in Tool Making

MAS Mechsol is an experienced custom mold and tool maker to reduce overall lead times in the supply chain.

  • MAS Mechsol's ability to build and run hot runner systems, challenging multi-cam molds, multi-action molds, 2K over-molds, and 3-plate molds set us apart from other plastic injection molders
  • Our expertise with MUD, Omni Mold, and Round Mate frames and inserts provide a high-quality process with a shortened overall lead time and allow you to bring your product to market quickly
  • MAS Mechsol's experienced molders use our state-of-the-art CNC 4 axis milling and lathe, precision EDM, high speed machining, hard milling, and IMMs to design plastic injection molds with quality and precision
  • MAS Mechsol's dedicated plastic injection mold inspection and assembly area allows for uninterrupted plastic manufacturing during the injection mold design and plastic tool building process
  • MAS Mechsol designs quick-turn synthetic tooling, limited production aluminum tooling, and semi or fully hardened production tooling, depending on your annual production requirements
  • Fully hardened production tools can be guaranteed up to 3 million cycles and have been known to last much longer!
  • MAS Mechsol stores, maintains, and repairs molds at our facility throughout the life of the mold.

Combining our various molding methods with fully automated, semi-automated, or manual assembly allows MAS Mechsol to be a turn-key plastic manufacturer and a one-stop shop.
