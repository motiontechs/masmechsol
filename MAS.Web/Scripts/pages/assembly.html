Packaging, Assembly & Secondary Operations

MAS Mechsol packages, assembles, and performs secondary operations to help shorten the supply chain, provide turnkey manufacturing, and reduce lead times and overall cost.

  • In-cycle assembly and in-house assembly lines available to shorten supply chain
  • On-site Automation Lab can build and implement custom robotics to help minimize labor costs
  • Decorating and packaging options are also available to make MAS Mechsol a one-stop shop solution

MAS Mechsol adds flexibility by offering many different combinations of assembly options, ranging from fully manual to fully automated, so that we can produce your plastic injection molded part within your time and budget restraint.
